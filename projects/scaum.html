SCAUM

This documentary explores the operations of an online drug scammer, delving into the mechanisms and strategies used to exploit individuals seeking medications or substances. Through interviews and observational footage, the film examines the intersection of technology, desperation, and deception. It attempts to shed light on how anonymity in digital spaces facilitates fraud while revealing the personal and systemic consequences for victims and perpetrators alike.

By focusing on the human story behind the transactions, the documentary offers an a-critical perspective on the socio-economic and psychological dimensions of online scams.
