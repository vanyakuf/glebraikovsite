Hi, I’m Gleb

I’m a PhD candidate at the Pierre Naville Center, the reference research institute for sociology at the University of Evry-Paris Saclay. My research merges ethnographic methods with visual and filmic sociology, using documentary filmmaking as a tool for sociological exploration.

In addition to my academic work, I operate as an autonomous videography unit, equipped with all necessary gear, and am eager to diversify my filmmaking experience. I specialize in backstage and reportage-style shoots, with an emphasis on capturing authentic communicative exchanges between subjects, highlighting their personal stories and interpersonal dynamics.
