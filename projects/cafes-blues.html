Café blues TEASER

What does “listening” mean in the context of cancer? Through an immersion in the “cafés conviviaux” of the Ligue contre le cancer, this documentary explores the role of listening volunteers, those discreet but essential players in the support of patients and their loved ones.

Who are these volunteers? What kind of solidarity is built up in these spaces? How do these interactions transform both patients and their carers?

Through filmed observations and interviews, this film examines contemporary forms of volunteer work in cancerology and the meaning of social ties in the face of illness.
